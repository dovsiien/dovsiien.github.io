UI/UX Design

My name is Ekaterina Drobina and I’m a UX and UI Designer from Russia living in Vancouver.

I’m a third-year SIAT SFU student with a concentration in Design which focuses on practices related to technology and its’ accessibility in design. I, as a designer, while studying preferred such areas as web development, graphic design, and UI design combining theoretical and practical knowledge. And with all the experience I got in SFU, I found myself passionate about UX design which includes not only the design itself but psychology and social studies as well, which is my second field of interest. Interface design and web development from the UI/UX perceptive is the best way for me to express my creativity as well as make the design accessible and affordable for users.

Photoshop/Illustrator/InDesign Figma/Framer Typography/graphic design skills HTML/CSS/JavaScript & Bootstrap Sketching Wireframing and Prototyping Research Interaction design Communication skills/Empathy
